## Project Philosophy: The Tome
Tome is not just a productivity app; it is a **Collective Intelligence Archive**.
- **The Brain Dump**: The ephemeral entrance. Raw, messy, and fast.
- **The Daily Plan**: The focus of the "Now". Action-oriented.
- **The Tome**: The permanent destination. A scholarly, scholarly digital brain of everything the user knows and thinks.

### Design Principles
- **Aesthetic**: Hyper-refined dark mode, "Monolith" interfaces, and "Linear-style" precision.
- **Speed**: Everything should feel instant. On-device AI (LiteRT/Compromise) handles the front line.
- **Privacy**: Personal thoughts are sacred. Process locally whenever possible.

## Technical Stack
- **Framework**: Expo (React Native)
- **Backend**: Convex (Real-time sync & AI Actions)
- **Auth**: Clerk (Secure user isolation)
- **NLP**: Hybrid (Compromise on-device, OpenAI in Cloud)
- **UI**: Tailwind (Uniwind) + Reanimated + Outfit Typography
